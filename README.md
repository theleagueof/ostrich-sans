Ostrich Sans & Ostrich Sans Inline
========
_by [Tyler Finck](http://www.finck.co)_

A gorgeous modern sans-serif with a very long neck. With a whole slew of styles & weights:

- Dashed (thin)
- Rounded (medium)
- Ultra light
- Normal
- Bold (race track style double lines)
- Black
- Inline (with italics)
- Heavy (round)
